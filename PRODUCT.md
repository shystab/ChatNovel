# Product

## Register

product

## Users

NovelCat is used by a small invited group of friends who write long-form fiction, manage chapters, share selected work, and occasionally discuss writing together. Users may spend hours in the editor, so the interface must stay calm and legible during long sessions.

## Product Purpose

NovelCat provides one private workspace for drafting novels, organizing chapters, consulting external references, collaborating with AI, and sharing selected writing with trusted friends. Success means users can open the app and begin writing without setup friction or interface confusion.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, capable, personal. NovelCat should feel cared for and slightly playful without becoming decorative or childish.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards made from repeated floating cards.
- Heavy glassmorphism that obscures text or competes with the writing surface.
- Warm beige paper themes applied to every page by default.
- Loud AI-purple gradients and decorative animation.
- Social feeds designed around engagement rather than trusted friends.

## Design Principles

- Keep the writing task visually dominant.
- Use familiar product controls and predictable navigation.
- Let personality appear in small details, not persistent decoration.
- Separate private work, AI assistance, and friend-facing content clearly.
- Make every empty, loading, and error state explain the next useful action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for controls and body text. Keep keyboard workflows usable, provide visible focus states, respect reduced motion and reduced transparency preferences, and avoid relying on color alone for status.
